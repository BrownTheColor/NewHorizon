About Us?

New Horizons Behavioral Health is a privately owned counseling center, conveniently located in Mesa, Az. Our talented and dedicated team of clinicians and staff are here to help you sort through issues that are impacting your quality of life. Together we can help you make the positive changes in your life that we know you are capable of achieving. Having someone to guide and encourage us along the way makes our journey a little easier and more productive. Let us help you reach greater heights.

New Horizons Vision Statment

New Horizons mission is to serve as a community resource and employer of choice by providing high-quality behavioral and addiction treatment to those in need. We take pride in providing efficient, quality, and affordable services by maintaining the clients continuity of care through a holistic, compassionate, and accessible approach to all. Our vision is to deliver outstanding clinical care and meet each client’s needs on an individual basis.

Services

But not limited to:


Individual Therapy

Joint process between a therapist and a person in therapy. Common goals of therapy can be to inspire change or improve quality of life.

Group Therapy

A group of patients meet to describe and discuss their problems together under the supervision of a therapist.

Anger Management

Goal-oriented therapeutic strategy that targets the emotional response to external factors that cause anger.

Basic Skills Training

Services are rehabilitative mental health services interventions designed to reduce cognitive and behavioral impairments and restore recipients to their highest level of functioning.

Anxiety Disorders

Treatment for a Health disorder characterized by feelings of worry, anxiety, or fear that are strong enough to interfere with one's daily activities

Bipolar/Mood Disorders

Treatment for a disorder associated with episodes of mood swings ranging from depressive lows to manic highs.

Bullying

Bullying Prevention

Food and Clothing Pantry

We distribute bags or boxes of food and clothes directly to those in need who reside in a specified area.

Housing Resources

Programs that provide shelter and supportive services for homeless individuals with the goal of helping them live independently and transition into permanent housing.

Social Skill Building for those who have been incarcerated

Skills we use to communicate and interact with each other, both verbally and non-verbally, through gestures, body language and our personal appearance.

Cognitive Disorder

Category of mental health disorders that primarily affect cognitive abilities including learning, memory, perception, and problem solving.

Depression

Feelings of severe despondency and dejection.

Domestic Violence /Child Abuse

Comprehensive treatment for domestic violence and child abuse through workshops that focus on specific areas that will help individuals heal after abuse. In an individual or group setting we talk about understanding and identifying triggers, establishing healthy relationships boundaries and more.

PTSD/Trauma

Treatment of a mental health condition that's triggered by a terrifying event — either experiencing it or witnessing it. Symptoms may include flashbacks, nightmares and severe anxiety.

Personality Disorders

Treatment of a type of mental disorder in which you have a rigid and unhealthy pattern of thinking, functioning and behaving.
